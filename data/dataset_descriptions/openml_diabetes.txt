The Pima Indians Diabetes Database contains 768 instances with 8 numeric-valued attributes and 1 class variable. The class variable represents whether a patient is tested positive (1) or negative (0) for diabetes. The dataset is balanced, with 500 instances in the negative class and 268 instances in the positive class. There are no missing values. The attributes are:
1. Number of times pregnant
2. Plasma glucose concentration a 2 hours in an oral glucose tolerance test
3. Diastolic blood pressure (mm Hg)
4. Triceps skin fold thickness (mm)
5. 2-Hour serum insulin (mu U/ml)
6. Body mass index (weight in kg/(height in m)^2)
7. Diabetes pedigree function
8. Age (years)
